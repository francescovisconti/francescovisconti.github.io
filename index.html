I am a __Researcher__ at the (Institute for Research on Population and Social Policies (IRPPS))[https://www.irpps.cnr.it)] of the __National Research Council__ (CNR) and an Adjunct Professor of Data Analysis at the University of Milan.
My work focuses on comparative politics, European Union, immigration, public opinion, solidarity, and representation.
